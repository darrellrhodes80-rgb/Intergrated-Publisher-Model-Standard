## I. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY: TURNING LIABILITY INTO ASSET

The current environment of Generative AI content creation is fundamentally blocked by legal and trust infrastructure failure. This prevents the adoption of AI for serious policy, technical, and creative work because the **origin of the data cannot be verified**, creating unacceptable copyright and hallucination liability.

**The Integrated Publisher Model (IPM)** solves this by introducing a **Hybrid Editorial Model**:
1.  **AI generates** the raw "clay" (drafts, code, policy).
2.  **Human experts verify** the output (The "Coherence Threshold").
3.  **The Protocol stamps** the content with a **Cryptographic Proof of Contribution (CPC)**.

This transforms the Publisher from a gatekeeper of "taste" into a **guarantor of truth**.

* **Copyright Liability:** Without proof of human modification, content may not be copyrightable (USCO guidance).
* **Hallucination Liability:** In technical or policy fields, unverified AI errors can cause catastrophic failure.

## III. THE SOLUTION: THE INTEGRATED MODEL

### 1. The Data Provenance Shield
The IPM acts as a firewall. No content passes to the final "Published" state without a digital signature from a qualified human editor. This signature serves as the **Cryptographic Proof of Contribution (CPC)**, legally differentiating the final asset from the raw AI prompt.

### 2. The Hybrid Editorial Model (The "Home Superstars")
We utilize a distributed workforce of subject-matter experts (veterans, retired professionals) to serve as the "Human in the Loop."
* **Role:** They do not write from scratch; they **audit, correct, and verify** AI output.
* **Result:** High-volume output with human-grade accuracy.

### 3. The Three-Way Payment Split
To sustain this ecosystem, revenue is algorithmically divided:
* **33% to the Architect/Author** (The Idea Originator)
* **33% to the Auditor/Editor** (The Verification Layer)
* **33% to the Platform** (The Infrastructure)
